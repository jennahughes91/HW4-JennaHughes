## Formatting Notes

### Title block
- Title: "CRM Meeting Report" — always this exact phrase, large and bold
- Date and Location appear directly below, each on its own line with a bold label

### Participants table
- Always three columns: **Name**, **Title**, **Company**
- List all attendees — both sides of the meeting
- If your company name is not mentioned in the notes, leave the Company cell blank (do not guess or invent it)
- Row order: customer/prospect attendees first, then your-side attendees

### Key Topics Discussed
- 3–6 bullet points
- Each bullet is a complete, standalone sentence (not a fragment)
- Factual and neutral in tone — avoid editorializing ("great meeting!") or speculation
- If a concrete next step or action item was agreed upon, include it as the final bullet, bolded with "Next step:" prefix
- Do not include everything from the notes verbatim — synthesize and summarize

### Footer
- Always: "Prepared by: Claude  |  Report generated: [today's date in Month DD, YYYY format]"
- Separated from the body by a thin horizontal rule
